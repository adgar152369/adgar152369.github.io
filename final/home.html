About Us

We are a motor scooter rental company located on the island of Cozumel off the coast of Mexico in the western Caribbean Sea. Scoots company caters to locals, walk-in tourists, enthusiasts, and cruise-based touring groups and partnerships. We help those "do their homework" before they arrive by providing information about the island, our services, and rentals. Come and enjoy touring the beautiful island of Cozumel!
